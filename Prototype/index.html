Facilitating successful brainstorming sessions

R541 Major Project - Qian Linlin

Welcome to this self-paced e-learning course about facilitating successful brainstorming session. In the game industry, creativity is always key. It is important for game designers to enable everyone in the team to contribute their best ideas to early game concept development. One important skill for them to acquire is facilitating brainstorming sessions.

Learning objectives
  • Reflect on their past experiences of facilitating brainstorming sessions: what worked well, and what didn’t? what contributed to that?
  • Identify what worked well in the Pixar Animation’s brainstorming practice, relating to the success factors introduced in the course
  • Develop authentic work scenarios that require the successful application of facilitation skills for brainstorming sessions
  • Write a plan for a brainstorming session following the checklist provided in the course
    • Who is this for?

    Anyone who needs to lead or facilitate brainstorming sessions at work or life. If you work with creative media such as games, you may find the context and examples more relevant. However, the approaches and tools shared in this course are applicable to all contexts.
